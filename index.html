This is the A11y Rules Podcast Network. A collection of podcasts about accessibility and inclusion.

The word "A11y" is a numeronym of the word accessibility. A numeronym is when you take the first and last letters of a word, count how many letters are in between, and put them together. There are 11 letters between the first a and the last y. So we get a-11-y, or a11y. I wrote a bit more about my choice of using a11y for my show name on the About page.
